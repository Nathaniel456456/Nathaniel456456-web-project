Kawasaki ZX10R 2017

A high-performance super bike engineered for SPEED, CONTROL, and PRECISION!!!

About the ZX10R

The Kawasaki ZX10R is a high-performance street bike powered by a 1000cc engine capable of leaving many high powered vehicles in the dust. Despite its massive power, the bike is equipped with advanced technology that provides a perfect balance of performance and control.

Kawasaki's engineering blends MOTOGP inspired electronics with precision handling, making the ZX10R one of most feared superbikes in the world.

Suspension

  • 43mm upside-down fork
  • Rear Mono shock

Traction Control System

The ZX10R features a highly advanced traction control system derived from MOTOGP as well as the Kawasaki Race Team (KRT). This system analyzes multiple performance parameters to deliver optimal grip, stability, and rider confidence.

  1. Front & Rear wheel speed
  2. Engine RPM
  3. Throttle position
  4. Rear wheel slippage
